Add Truck Image to LOGIFLOW

Instructions:

1. Right-click on the truck image from your screenshot
2. Select "Save image as..."
3. Navigate to: C:\Users\aliah\Downloads\LOGIFLOW\LOGIFLOW-main\public\
4. Save it as: truck-image.jpg
5. Refresh your LOGIFLOW application

Target path: LOGIFLOW-main/public/truck-image.jpg

Once you save the image, it will automatically appear in your Container Tracking section!
